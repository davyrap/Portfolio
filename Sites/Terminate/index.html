Terminate

Terminate is a story-driven videogame where you have to solve various puzzles and challeneges in order to discover new of a story. You are a software engineer living in the year 1967. You work for the Ziblax Softworks, one of the first companies working in this field. One day, you logged in your system just like you did for the last 6 years, but something was off. You just didn't know it yet...
